About House of Needy

We are dedicated to empowering the needy in Ghana through vocational training and healthcare support. Our mission is to provide sustainable skills and essential medical aid to those in need, helping them build a better future for themselves and their communities.

Our Work

Vocational Training

We support skilling expenses for tailoring vocational trainees, providing them with the tools and knowledge to become self-sufficient.

Healthcare Support

We provide medical equipment to help combat the outbreak of viral ailments, ensuring access to essential healthcare services.
